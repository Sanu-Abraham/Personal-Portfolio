Who I Am

Student. Artist. Thinker. Developer.

Hi, I am Sanu...

I like to describe myself as, most of all, a lifelong learner. Rarely am I fixed on a particular subject for long. I am endlessly curious to learn about new things, explore new ideas, build new things, and connect with new people.

This website exists as both a portfolio and an archive: a place to document what I build, what I think about, and what I'm becoming. I want to share the work that I'm proud of, ideas and thoughts that I find interesting, and simple things that make me, me.

As of April 2026, I have completed 12th grade from Carmel International School. I don't really have a specific plan for the future, but I am excited to explore different paths and see where my curiosity takes me. I enjoy art, music, books, cinema, drama, performance, math, science, technology, coding, design, and problem-solving (not saying I'm good at these, but I like them).
